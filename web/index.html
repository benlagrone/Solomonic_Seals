Not a prophecy engine, and not a replacement for ordinary wisdom.

  • It does not promise guaranteed outcomes.
  • It does not outrank prayer, conscience, or community.
  • It is meant to strengthen attention, obedience, and follow-through.

FAQ

Common questions about True Vine OS.

What is True Vine OS?

It is a biblical wisdom guide and Life OS that combines a live daily clock, Scripture anchors, source-faithful texts, and reflective practice in one web companion.

What makes it different from a journal or planner?

The focus is not generic tracking. It is the movement from counsel to action to examination, with Scripture and source visibility kept close to the experience.

Who should start with the guide page?

Anyone new to the product should begin with the daily rhythm guide, then open the clock once the instrument’s purpose is clear.

Does it replace prayer or counsel?

No. It is built to support prayerful attention and disciplined follow-through, not to replace conscience, ordinary obedience, or wise counsel.

Start with the guide if you are new. Open the live clock when you are ready to read the day.
